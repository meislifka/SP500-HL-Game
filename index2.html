Welcome to the S&P 500 Stock Price Guessing Game!


HOW THE GAME WORKS:
You are given the name, ticker symbol, and trend of 2 stocks but only the price for the second one.
You must determine if the stock is higher or lower than the stock with the given price.

HOW TO PLAY:
Click ▲ to indicate that the first stock price is HIGHER than the second.
Click ▼ to indicate that the first stock price is LOWER than the second.
